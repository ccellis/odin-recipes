Salt and Straw Ice Cream

My favorite ice cream base recipe. Easy, fast, no eggs, no custard, takes mixins/flavors well. Don't skip the xanthan gum-- it really maximizes the smoothness.

Ingredients

  • 115g granulated sugar
  • 15g dry milk powder
  • 1g xanthan gum
  • 40g light corn syrup
  • 330g whole milk
  • 330g heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Combine the dry ingredients and mix well
  2. Combine the corn syrup and milk in a pot.
  3. Add the sugar mixture and whisk vigorously until smooth.
  4. Set the pot over medium heat and cook until the sugar has fully dissolved, about 3 minutes. Stir often and adjust heat to avoid a simmer.
  5. Remove the pot from the heat and add the cream. Whisk until fully combined.
  6. Store mixture in an airtight container and refrigerate until well chilled, at least 6 hours but ideally at least 24.
  7. Churn chilled ice cream base according to your ice cream maker's directions.
